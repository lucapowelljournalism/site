Hi there, my name is Luca, I'm a data reporter with a passion for stories related to public health, policing, and education. I'm a coding nerd with a writing background. In 2021, I was selected by RFA to work at the Traverse City Record-Eagle, where I've reported on hospitals failing to screen the mentally ill, PPP money being diverted to for-profit charter school management companies, AirBNBs gobbling up housing, and COVID-19 particles in our poo.

Before RFA, I was a freelance reporter in New York City. I'm a graduate of the CUNY Graduate School of Journalism in New York City.
