Luca Powell is a reporter producing investigative features on issues of policing and public health.

Luca is also a data journalist passionate about scraping information from public sources, building data visualizations, and designing interactive stories that broaden our definition of journalism.

Luca is a recent graduate of the CUNY Graduate School of Journalism in New York City. As of 2021, he's freelancing stories in NYC.

Luca is originally from New Haven, CT. He's a dual Italian-American citizen and a former English teacher.
