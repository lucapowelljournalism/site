Luca Powell is a reporter producing investigative features on issues of policing, immigration and public health. More broadly, I get excited about finding new ways into citywide stories of the day. I've localized compelling public health stories to New York and captured underreported aspects of America's immigration system.

Luca is also a strong data journalist with experience building interactives stories, ranging from simple click-through storytelling to websites that tap APIs in real-time, to scraping websites. In November, Luca contracted with the AP's election's scraping team to pull in presidential vote totals from hundreds of county websites in real time.

Luca is a recent graduate of the CUNY Graduate School of Journalism in New York City. As of 2021, he's freelancing stories in New York City and looking for full-time reporting work.

About me! I'm originally from New Haven, CT. I'm a native Italian speaker, and a very strong French speaker as well. Before J-school, I worked as an English teacher in Vietnam for several years, an experience which has influenced a lot of my reporting so far.
